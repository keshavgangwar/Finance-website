Enable Concatenated SMS

Each SMS is limited to 150 characters. The SMS protocol supports only 150 characters (actually it's 160 but some are used for concatenate the message) for each sms sent from an international SMS gateway.

But, you can still send longer SMS up to 1050 characters. The SMS gateway will split the large SMS into smaller pieces and then join them at the recipient's handset which is the mobile number so the recipient only sees 1 SMS.

If you Enable Concatenated SMS below, you can send SMS up to 1050 characters. You will be charged per 150 characters.. For example, if you send a SMS that has 200 characters, you will be charged for 2 SMS.

Using Your Local SMS Provider

We can also integrate your local SMS provider. Please send us their website, API documentation, and authentication details such as username/password, API key, and Sender Id at support@loandisk.com. You would pay your SMS provider directly but SMS will be sent from Loandisk. We do not charge anything for this service.

It will take us 1 week to integrate this. To speed up the process, send us a test mobile number so we can check if the integration is working.

Please note that we are under no obligation to integrate your SMS gateway and we may decide not to integrate it as per our discretion.
